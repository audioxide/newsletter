Monthly Roundup // March 02020
Pfft, what an uneventful month. Not a single current event to reference. Not one.

We kicked March off with Plastic Beach by Gorillaz, which celebrated its 10th birthday. It's a cracking album. Probably not the band's best, but almost certainly its most fun. Should we ever find ourselves in unusual and unsettling times it may be just the thing to tune out to.

That was followed up with two new releases. First, A Written Testimony by Jay Electronica. After 576 years in development it didn't quite live up to the hype, although it's still likely to be one of our favourite releases of the year... if we make it to the end of the year. Finally, Uneasy Laughter by Moaning. Meh.

Plus two more entries in the not terribly funny Funnyfarm series. The band get an agent then skip town. What on earth could happen next?

Stay safe,

André, Fred, Andrew, and Marcus
